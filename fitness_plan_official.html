What to Expect

  • Week 1: Building the habit is the only goal. Nail the schedule.
  • Week 2: Exams — maintenance only. Walk, eat clean, don't spiral.
  • Weeks 3–4: Back on track. Energy improves, foundation builds.
  • Weeks 5–6: Visible change begins. Most people notice a difference here.
  • Weeks 7–10: Most significant body composition change. Push hardest here.
